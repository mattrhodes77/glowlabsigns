Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the entire process take? +
Standard timeline is 8-10 weeks from approved design to installation. This includes design consultation (1-3 days), custom fabrication (4-6 weeks), shipping (2-3 weeks), and professional installation (1 day). Rush options may be available for an additional fee.
Do you handle permits and electrical work? +
Yes! Professional installation includes all necessary electrical work by licensed electricians. We coordinate permit applications where required (typically for commercial installations and exterior signage). This is all included in your quoted price.
Can these be used outdoors? +
Our current signs are designed for indoor use only. We're developing weatherproof outdoor options - contact us if you have a specific outdoor project in mind.
What's included in the warranty? +
Every sign includes a 5-year warranty covering LED modules, electrical components, and structural integrity. This covers manufacturing defects and normal wear. Installation warranty covers mounting and electrical work for 2 years.
Can I change my design after approval? +
Design changes are free during the consultation phase. Once fabrication begins, changes may incur additional costs and timeline delays depending on the extent of changes. We work hard to ensure you're 100% happy with the design before production starts.
What are the payment terms? +
We require 50% deposit upon design approval to begin fabrication, 40% when your sign ships, and final 10% upon successful installation. We accept wire transfer, check, or credit card (3% processing fee for cards).
Do you install outside of California? +
Yes! We coordinate professional installation nationwide through our network of licensed electricians and installers. Travel costs for installations outside Northern California are included in your quote.
How much does it cost to run the sign? +
LED signs are extremely energy efficient. A typical 6×6 ft sign uses about 200-300 watts (similar to 3-4 light bulbs). Running 8 hours/day costs approximately $10-15/month depending on your electricity rates.
Do you have an iPhone app? +
We're actively developing one and hope to launch one soon. If this is critical for your project, contact us if you're interested in beta testing.
